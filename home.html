Our History

Saint Joseph is a central figure in the Christian tradition, known for his role as the earthly father of Jesus Christ and the husband of the Virgin Mary. Although little is mentioned about him in the biblical accounts, Joseph's significance lies in his obedience to God's will and his unwavering faith.

Seeing and Savouring Christ

"Seeing and Savouring Christ" encapsulates the idea of deeply experiencing and finding delight in the person and teachings of Jesus Christ. It emphasizes the act of perceiving and appreciating Christ's presence, character, and teachings, leading to a profound sense of joy and satisfaction. It invites individuals to develop a close relationship with Christ and to savor the transformative impact of encountering Him in their lives.

A God-Entanced Vision of All Things

"A God-Entranced Vision of All Things" encompasses the concept of perceiving and understanding the world through the lens of a deep reverence and awe for God. It suggests that when we view all aspects of life, from the ordinary to the extraordinary, with a God-centered perspective, we gain a heightened appreciation for the divine presence and purpose in everything. This vision prompts us to recognize God's handiwork, seek His wisdom, and align our lives with His greater plan, resulting in a profound and transformative perspective on the world around us.
